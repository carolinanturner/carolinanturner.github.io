First Bagel

Bagels! They are yummy! Here are some savory bagel flavors: everything flavored, asiago cheese flavored, sesame seed, onion flavored, garlic flavored, sea salt flavored. You can also add a breakfast meat to make it more savory such as bacon, ham, sausage, canadian bacon, turkey bacon, maybe some eggs, or turkey sausage. Don't forget the cream cheese! Chive and onion is a fan favorite but you could also try a garlic flavored cream cheese, garden vegetable, or just opt for some old fashioned salted butter.

Second Bagel

I admit I am truly a sugar addict. I would have dessert for breakfast if it weren't against all social norms. The closest thing I could get away with is a sweet bagel (if not something else like a cinnamon roll or breakfast pastry). My favorite would be cinnamon raisin flavored but theres also blueberry, strawberry, brown sugar cinnamon, and plain. I am currently snacking on a bagel with blueberry flavored philadelphia cream cheese, drizzled with local organic honey and sea salt. It may have a lot of sugar, but it makes me happy and is my favorite snack to have while devloping websites for CSCE: 242. Happy munching!!
